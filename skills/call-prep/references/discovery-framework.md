# Discovery Framework Reference

## How to Use This File
This file provides the LOCAL scaffold for the discovery framework. ALWAYS search Notion for "discovery framework" and "qualification criteria" to get the current version. ALWAYS search Google Drive Esker folder for "How Esker Sells Esker" to reference Esker's discovery methodology.

## BANT Framework (Skeleton)
- **Budget**: Has budget been allocated? What's the approval process? Who signs?
- **Authority**: Who is the decision maker? Who else needs to be involved?
- **Need**: What's the business pain? How urgent? What happens if they do nothing?
- **Timeline**: When do they need a solution? What's driving the timeline?

## 3Ps Qualification (Skeleton)
- **Pain**: What specific pain are they experiencing in AP/AR?
- **Power**: Who has the power to approve and champion this?
- **Process**: What's their buying process? Evaluation steps? Legal/procurement?

## Esker Discovery Topics
- Current AP/AR workflow (manual vs. automated)
- Invoice volume (AP and AR)
- ERP system and version
- Current cost per invoice
- DSO/DPO and working capital goals
- Team size in AP/AR
- Pain points: errors, delays, compliance, vendor complaints
- Strategic initiatives: digital transformation, cost reduction, cash management
